# Electricity Consumption vs Renewable Energy in Singapore

## Overview
This project analyzes the relationship between Singapore's electricity consumption and renewable energy generation using historical data. The objective is to investigate consumption trends, evaluate renewable energy growth, and determine whether renewable energy production has the potential to meet electricity demand or "break even" over time.

## Objectives
1. Analyze historical electricity consumption in Singapore.
2. Analyze the growth of renewable energy capacity.
3. Compare electricity demand against renewable energy production.
4. Identify long-term trends and seasonal patterns.
5. Forecast future electricity consumption.
6. Estimate whether renewable energy generation can eventually meet electricity demand.

## How it was performed
1. Data cleaning and preprocessing to select relevant datasets
2. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
3. Time series decomposition
4. Trend and seasonal analysis
5. Linear regression modelling
6. Forecasting electricity demand
7. Comparison between projected renewable generation and electricity demand
8. Suggestions on actions for different stakeholders
9. Conclusion
